Civil Litigation

The law firm's attorneys represent both plaintiffs and defendants in state and federal courts throughout California. We have litigated a diverse array of cases including personal injury/wrongful death claims, environmental issues, construction defects, business disputes, insurance claims, agriculture, employment and education related disputes.

Personal Injury Litigation

The law firm represents both plaintiffs and defendants in all types of personal injury matters, including claims arising from motor vehicle accidents, wrongful deaths, toxic torts, professional liability, premises liability and product liability matters. The firm's attorneys have a particular wealth of experience both in prosecuting and defending actions involving punitive and exemplary damages in the multi-million dollar range.

Insurance Defense

The firm’s insurance defense practice has represented insurance companies and their insureds for over two decades. Parichan, Renberg & Crossman’s insurance defense actions include all manner of vehicle accidents, premises liability, environmental liability, construction defect, professional liability, fire loss, product liability, property and casualty insurance. We also provides representation for claims involving third party litigation and subrogation. The attorneys have developed cost-effective, efficient defenses to the claims confronting the insurance clients and their insureds.

Product Liability Litigation

Parichan, Renberg & Crossman’s attorneys have handled a broad range of product liability actions regarding motor vehicles, in addition to cases involving defective machinery, agricultural products, aircraft, playground equipment, power tools, recreational sports equipment, medical products, consumer products, chemicals and pesticides. We provides aggressive, cost-effective solutions to resolve complex product liability disputes.

Construction Defects Litigation

For more then 20 years the law firm’s attorneys have represented developers and subcontractors in various construction defect cases. The cases involve also every type of construction project; including single family homes, subdivision drainage and roads, and large commercial buildings and pipelines.

Insurance Coverage Disputes

The attorneys of the firm have assisted individuals and business with insurance coverage disputes that have arose from claims related to various types of insurance policies, including commercial general liability, boiler and machinery, product contamination, vehicle, and theft policies. The attorneys work with the policy holders to obtain insurance coverage that is owed by the insurance company.

Business & Commercial Litigation

Parichan, Renberg & Crossman provides representation for clients in complex business and commercial litigation matters, including contract disputes, trade secrets, fraud and negligent misrepresentation, business torts, and toxic torts. The attorneys represent business clients spanning from major corporations to small local businesses.

Agricultural Law

Parichan, Renberg & Crossman assists clients in matters involving agricultural law, including contract disputes, business disputes, farm agreements, farm equipment cases, pesticide and agricultural chemical litigation. The firm's experienced lawyers work together in overlapping issues involving business and commercial litigation, personal injury claims, product liability and toxic torts.

Mediation & Arbitration

Parichan, Renberg & Crossman understands that not all legal issues are best resolved through aggressive litigation, and has developed and implemented comprehensive strategies to help clients obtain amicable resolutions, avoiding costly court battles. Attorneys at the firm have over 20 years of experience in alternative dispute resolution, and have served as judicially-appointed arbitrators, mediators and settlement conference judges in a wide range of disputes.
