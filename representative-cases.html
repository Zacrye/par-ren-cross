Personal Injury Cases

Truck vs Work at Construction Site

Result: Settled for $4.2 million, Fresno County Superior Court

A 42 years old foreman was working for the general contractor at a work site on Highway 99. He was supervising a crew that was performing shoulder work that involving applying asphalt. A truck containing asphalt material was backing towards a spreading machine when it hit the plaintiff, knocked him to the ground and the rear tire ran over his hip and thigh. Plaintiff sustained substantial injuries, including fractured femur that needed a surgical rod and nerve injuries to his leg.

Auto vs Pedestrian

Result: Verdict of $1.3 million, Fresno County Superior Court

Represented a 22 year old woman who was a student at Fresno State University. She was walking on a sidewalk near the library when a person backing his pick-up truck jumped the curb and hit knocked her to the ground. She sustained a fractured hip and had orthopedic problems with her legs.
